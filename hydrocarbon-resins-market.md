<p><p>The hydrocarbon resins market is segmented into several types based on their chemical composition. C5 resins, derived from pentane fractions, are known for their excellent compatibility and low odor. Aliphatic resins provide transparency and maintain flexibility in applications. C9 resins, originating from nonane fractions, are valued for their strong adhesion properties. C5/C9 resins combine features of both, enhancing versatility and performance. Aliphatic/Aromatic resins blend aliphatic and aromatic characteristics, offering balanced properties suited for diverse applications in adhesives, coatings, and sealants.</p></p>
