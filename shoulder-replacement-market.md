<p><ul><li>Anatomical Shoulder Prosthesis</li><li>Reverse Shoulder Prosthesis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shoulder replacement market primarily consists of two main types: anatomical shoulder prosthesis and reverse shoulder prosthesis. Anatomical shoulder prosthesis mimics the natural structure of the shoulder, preserving its anatomy and functioning well in patients with intact rotator cuffs. In contrast, reverse shoulder prosthesis alters the shoulder's mechanics, making it suitable for patients with severe rotator cuff injuries or arthritis. Both types aim to relieve pain and restore mobility, catering to diverse patient needs and conditions.</p></p>
