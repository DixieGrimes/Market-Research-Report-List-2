<p><ul><li>For Raised Floor</li><li>For Non-Raised Floor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cold Aisle Containment (CAC) systems are designed to enhance cooling efficiency in data centers. The market can be divided into two main types: raised floor and non-raised floor systems. Raised floor CAC systems utilize elevated floors to manage airflow, helping to direct cool air from underfloor units into cold aisles while preventing mixing with warm air. Non-raised floor CAC systems rely on ceiling-mounted or wall-mounted cooling solutions, making them suitable for environments without a raised floor, optimizing temperature control effectively.</p></p>
