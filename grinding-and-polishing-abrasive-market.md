<p><p>The grinding and polishing abrasive market is categorized into three main types: bonded abrasives, coated abrasives, and super abrasives. Bonded abrasives are made by binding abrasive grains into a solid form, ideal for grinding applications. Coated abrasives consist of abrasive grains adhered to a flexible substrate, commonly used for sanding and finishing surfaces. Super abrasives, comprising diamond and cubic boron nitride, offer exceptional performance for precision grinding and cutting applications, primarily in specialized industrial sectors.</p></p>
