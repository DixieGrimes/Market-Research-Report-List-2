<p><strong>The Gene Delivery System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Viral Gene Delivery System</li><li>Non-viral Gene Delivery System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gene delivery system market comprises two primary types: viral and non-viral gene delivery systems. Viral gene delivery utilizes modified viruses to transport genetic material into host cells, leveraging the natural ability of viruses to infect cells efficiently. Conversely, non-viral gene delivery employs methods such as liposomes, nanoparticles, and physical techniques like electroporation to facilitate gene transfer without using viruses. Each system presents unique advantages and challenges, influencing their applications in gene therapy and genetic research.</p></p>
