<p><p>Trace element monitoring and control equipment includes methods like Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy (AAS) and Electrochemical Analysis for detecting and quantifying trace elements in various samples. AAS involves measuring the absorption of light by vaporized sample atoms to assess metal concentrations, while Electrochemical Analysis utilizes electrical measurements to analyze ionic species in solutions. Both techniques are essential in environmental monitoring, food safety, and industrial applications, ensuring compliance with regulations and maintaining product quality by detecting harmful trace elements effectively.</p></p>
