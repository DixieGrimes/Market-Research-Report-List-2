<p><p>The radiology treatment equipment market encompasses three main categories: External Beam Radiotherapy Equipment, which delivers concentrated radiation from outside the body to target tumors; Internal Beam Radiotherapy Equipment, also known as brachytherapy, involves placing radioactive sources directly inside or near a tumor; and Systemic Radiotherapy Equipment, which uses radioactive substances administered via the bloodstream to treat cancer throughout the body. Each type serves specific therapeutic purposes, enhancing treatment outcomes and expanding options for cancer management.</p></p>
