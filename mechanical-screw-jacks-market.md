<p><strong>The Mechanical Screw Jacks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Worm Gear Screw Jack</li><li>Ball Screw Jack</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical screw jacks market primarily features two types: worm gear screw jacks and ball screw jacks. Worm gear screw jacks utilize a threaded worm to convert rotational motion into linear motion, offering high torque and compact designs suitable for heavy loads. In contrast, ball screw jacks use ball bearings for smoother operation and reduced friction, allowing for higher efficiency and faster movement. Both types serve critical roles in various industrial applications like lifting, positioning, and adjusting machinery.</p></p>
