<p><p>The Crossply OTR (Off-The-Road) tire market includes two main types: inner tube and tubeless tires. Inner tube tires feature a separate inflatable rubber tube inside the tire, providing added puncture resistance and easier repair. Conversely, tubeless tires are designed without an inner tube, sealing air directly within the tire, which reduces weight and prevents air loss due to punctures. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on operational needs, vehicle compatibility, and terrain conditions, influencing their demand in various applications.</p></p>
