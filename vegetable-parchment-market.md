<p><p>The vegetable parchment market includes two primary types: plain vegetable parchment and siliconized genuine vegetable parchment. Plain vegetable parchment is an untreated, versatile baking paper known for its greaseproof and moisture-resistant qualities, ideal for various food applications. In contrast, siliconized genuine vegetable parchment is coated with silicone, enhancing its non-stick properties and durability, making it suitable for high-temperature baking and intricate culinary tasks. Both types cater to the needs of food industries, from baking to food packaging.</p></p>
