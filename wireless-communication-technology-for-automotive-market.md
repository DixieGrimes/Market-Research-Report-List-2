<p><p>Wireless communication technology in the automotive market primarily includes Dedicated Short Range Communication (DSRC) and mesh networking. DSRC is a protocol designed for low-latency, high-reliability communication between vehicles and infrastructure, enhancing safety and traffic management through real-time data exchange. In contrast, mesh networking facilitates peer-to-peer communication among vehicles, enabling a decentralized network that improves coverage and robustness. Both technologies play crucial roles in developing intelligent transportation systems, contributing to enhanced safety, efficiency, and driver experience.</p></p>
