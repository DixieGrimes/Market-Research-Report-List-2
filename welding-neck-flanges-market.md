<p><p>Welding neck flanges are widely used in piping systems and are categorized into three main types based on their face designs: </p><p>1. **Raised Face (RF)**: Features a raised surface for effective sealing, commonly used in general applications.</p><p>2. **Flat Face (FF)**: Consists of a flat surface that provides a tighter fit against the mating surface, ideal for low-pressure applications.</p><p>3. **Ring Type Joint (RTJ)**: Designed for high-pressure environments, incorporating a groove and metal ring for enhanced sealing. These types cater to various industry requirements.</p></p>
