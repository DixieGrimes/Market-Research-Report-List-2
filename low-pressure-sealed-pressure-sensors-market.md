<p><p>The low pressure sealed pressure sensors market includes various types designed for specific applications. Strain type sensors measure pressure through deformation of a material when force is applied. Piezoresistive sensors utilize the change in electrical resistance in response to applied pressure, offering high sensitivity. Capacitive sensors detect pressure changes by measuring fluctuations in capacitance between electrodes. Other types may incorporate technologies like optical or resonant methods, catering to diverse industrial needs and enhancing measurement accuracy across a range of applications.</p></p>
