<p><strong>The Consumer 3D Vision Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structured Light</li><li>iToF</li><li>dToF</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Consumer 3D Vision Sensor market encompasses various technologies, primarily structured light, iToF (indirect Time of Flight), dToF (direct Time of Flight), and others. Structured light uses projected patterns to capture depth information, while iToF sensors measure the time it takes for light to reflect off surfaces indirectly, enabling depth sensing. dToF, on the other hand, measures the time taken for light to return directly to the sensor, facilitating accurate distance mapping. Other technologies may include hybrid systems or advanced sensing methods.</p></p>
