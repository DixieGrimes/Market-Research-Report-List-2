<p><p>The Non-asbestos Organic (NAO) brake pads market is primarily divided into two types: copper-free and copper-bearing brake pads. Copper-free brake pads are formulated using alternative materials like rubber and resins, offering reduced environmental impact and low noise levels. In contrast, copper-bearing brake pads contain copper fibers for enhanced performance and heat dissipation but may pose environmental concerns due to copper runoff. Both types cater to different consumer preferences balancing performance, safety, and ecological considerations in braking systems.</p></p>
